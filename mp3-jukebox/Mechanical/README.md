## Mechanical Design

The enclosure consists of a two-piece housing:

- `enclosure_bottom.stl`
- `enclosure_top.stl`

Custom-designed mounting components include:

- `dfplayer_mount_adapter.stl` – Secures the DFPlayer Mini module to the enclosure.
- `usb_c_mount.stl` – Mounting interface for the USB-C charging board.
- Internal support features integrated into the enclosure base.

Third-party mechanical components were used for:

- Screen holder – Thingiverse  
  https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2176764

- 6mm tactile button enclosure – Cults3D (CC BY-SA)  
  https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/tool/enclosure-for-6mm-tactile-button-switch
